Wise care Pharmaceutical is a leading Egyptian cosmeceutical company that was established in 2020 with its headquarters located in Mansoura city with the objective of providing high-quality and innovative products at the most appropriate price possible.

The Chairman of the Board of Directors, Mr Ahmed Gaber Nour El-Din, worked in the field of selling medicines in the Middle East Chemical Company, in the Medical Professions Branch in Mansoura, and grew up in the career from 1996 to 2011 from a sales representative to a branch sales supervisor. He obtained certificates of management of sales skills from more than one approved source. won the award of the first representative in sales at the level of the Republic on all branches of the Middle East in the year 2000. Then he made his way, founding kelza company in Saudi Arabia, Jeddah from 2011 to 2017, which became one of the most important distribution companies for medical preparations and equipment in Jeddah. after that, he founded Brand International Pharma in Mansoura and contributed to the establishment of the Brand International Factory for cosmetics and nutritional supplements. And currently, he established Wise Care, a company to manufacture medical preparations and nutritional supplements in 2020 With only one desire, which is providing high-quality and innovative products made in Egypt at the most appropriate price possible.

  • Competing for the imported products and nutritional supplements in quality and price.
  • Raise the title "made in Egypt".
  • Providing the consumer high-quality products at the most appropriate price.
  • We do our best to get rid of chemicals in our products replacing them with natural elements with the same effectiveness.
  • One of our goals is asuring that our products being safe and suitable for all ages and those with chronic diseases.
